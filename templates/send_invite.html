⚙️ Gmail Setup Guide

To send emails, you need a Gmail account with a 2-step App Password. Follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Google Account → Security → Enable 2-Step Verification if not already on.

  2. Go to Google Account → Security → App Passwords.
    (Search "App Passwords" in your Google account settings)

  3. Click Create, name it "DocTracker", and copy the 16-character password generated.

  4. In Railway → your web service → Variables, add these two:

    MAIL_SENDER = your Gmail address
    MAIL_PASSWORD = the 16-char App Password
    APP_URL = https://docktracker.up.railway.app

  5. Redeploy — email sending will be activated automatically.

💡 Tip: Use a dedicated Gmail account for the system (e.g. doctracker.deped.leyte@gmail.com) rather than your personal account.
