System Overview
Three Roles, One System

Every person in DocTracker has a clearly defined role. Clients can only see their own documents — the staff document table is never accessible to the public.

👑Administrator
System Admin
Manages the entire system — users, offices, QR codes, and configuration. Full visibility over all documents and all activity.
  • Invite and manage all staff accounts
  • Generate and print all office QR code sets
  • View, search and filter every document
  • Access full activity and audit log
  • Post App Welcome QR for public onboarding
🏢Office Staff
Staff Member
Receives, processes, routes, and releases documents. Creates the paper trail and maintains chain of custody across offices.
  • Receive documents via QR scan or manual entry
  • Update status, add remarks, edit details
  • Create batch routing slips with time filters
  • Use AI to auto-extract fields from photos/PDFs
  • View dashboard and all documents
👤Client
Client / Public
Submits documents and tracks their progress. Privacy-first — clients can only ever see their own submitted documents.
  • Register free — no email required
  • Submit documents to any registered office
  • Get a unique QR code receipt per document
  • Track real-time status and travel log
  • Cannot see other clients' documents
  • Cannot access the staff document table
End-to-End Workflow
Complete Process Flow

From system setup all the way to document release — every phase of the lifecycle explained step by step.

PHASE 01
System Initialization
👑 Admin
01.1
🔐
Admin Login
Admin logs in via /login using credentials set in Railway environment variables. Full access to all system functions — users, documents, QR codes, activity logs.
01.2
🖨️
Generate Office QR Sets
Goes to QR Codes page, types each office name, downloads four QR codes per office: App Welcome, SUB, REC, REL. Posts them in the correct physical locations.
01.3
📊
Monitor Everything
Admin sees all documents on the main dashboard with full search, date, and time filters. Can edit, reroute, or update any document at any time.
PHASE 02
Staff Onboarding
🏢 Admin → Staff
02.1
📧
Admin Sends Invitation
Admin goes to 👥 Users → 📧 Send Invite. System generates a one-time 32-char token with a 48-hour expiry. Brevo HTTPS API delivers a branded email.
02.2
📝
Staff Registers & QR Auto-Created
Staff clicks the link, fills in their Office/Unit name, full name, username, and password. The system immediately generates their office's full QR code set automatically.
02.3
🖨️
Print & Post QR Codes
Staff logs in, downloads their office QR set. Posts: SUB at the client counter, REC at the receiving window, REL at the releasing window.
02.4
📊
Access Dashboard
Staff lands on the document dashboard. Can log walk-in documents, scan QR codes, filter by date and time, and create routing slips for batch forwarding.
PHASE 03
Client Registration
👤 Client
03.1
📱
Scan the Welcome QR
Client spots the App Welcome QR posted at the office entrance or bulletin board. Scans it — opens this landing page directly on their phone.
03.2
✍️
Register Free
Taps Register, fills in full name, chooses a username and password (8+ chars, 1 number). Account created instantly — no email address needed.
03.3
🔒
Private Client Portal
Client logs in and lands on their personal portal. They see only their own submitted documents — the staff document table is completely inaccessible.
PHASE 04
Document Submission
👤 Client
04.1
📄
Choose Office & Fill Form
Taps Submit Here on any office card above. Form opens pre-filled with that office. Fills in unit/school, sender name, document particulars, category, and Referred To officer. Can add multiple documents to one batch.
04.2
💾
Logged to Database
Each document gets a unique Reference Number (REF-2026-XXXX) and 8-char system ID. Inserted into PostgreSQL JSONB with status Pending and an initial travel log entry.
04.3
📲
QR Code Receipt
Client is shown a QR code immediately after submission. Screenshot or download it — this QR is the key to receiving and releasing the document at the office.
04.4
👁️
Always in Your Portal
The QR is permanently available in each document's tracking page in the client portal. Tap any document → QR displayed with full status history and travel log.
PHASE 05
Document Receiving
👤 Client + 🏢 Staff
05.1
🚶
Client Arrives at Counter
Client brings physical documents to the office receiving window and shows their document QR code from their phone portal.
05.2
📷
Staff Scans Document QR
Staff uses the DocTracker scan page or the office REC scanner. System pulls up the document record instantly — no manual search required.
05.3
📥
Mark as Received
Staff taps Mark as Received. Status: Pending → Received. Receiving officer name, date, time, and office are appended to the travel log automatically.
05.4
⚡
Client Sees Update Instantly
The client can see the status change in their private portal immediately — no need to ask the staff. Full travel log entry is visible right away.
PHASE 06
Staff Processing & Routing
🏢 Staff / Admin
06.1
📊
View & Filter Dashboard
Staff filters by Date and Time range (e.g. 08:00–12:00) to isolate a morning batch, then searches by name or reference number. Clicks any row to open the full document detail.
06.2
🚌
Batch Route via Slip
Select documents on the dashboard, set destination office, click Create Routing Slip. Or use Auto-select by Time to select an entire shift's batch in one click. All selected documents → Routed.
06.3
🖨️
Print & Forward
System generates a routing slip (SLIP-2026-XXXX) with a document table, prepared-by and received-by signature blocks. Print and attach to the physical documents.
06.4
🤖
AI-Assisted Entry
For walk-in documents: upload a photo or PDF of the physical document. Claude AI extracts all fields automatically — name, sender, category, dates — and pre-fills the entire form.
PHASE 07
Document Release
🏢 Staff + 👤 Client
07.1
✅
Staff Sets Status to Released
When processing is complete, staff updates the document to Released using the Quick Status Update panel. Client sees this change immediately in their private portal.
07.2
📲
Client Presents QR for Pickup
Client returns to the office, opens their document from the portal, and shows the QR at the releasing window. Staff scans it to confirm pickup.
07.3
🎉
Lifecycle Complete
Travel log records the final entry. Client's portal shows the complete journey: Submitted → Received → In Review → Released, with every timestamp and officer name throughout.

📥
Received

🔍
In Review
Staff updates during processing
🚌
Routed
Routed to another office via slip
✅
Released
Client picks up — cycle complete
⚡ QR-Triggered Updates
Pending → Received — staff scans document QR at receiving counter

Released → Pickup confirmed — staff scans document QR at releasing window to close the lifecycle
✋ Manual Staff Updates
Any → In Review, Routed, On Hold, Archived

Routing slip creation automatically sets all selected documents to Routed in one action
🔒 Privacy by Design
Clients view the full travel log of their own documents only. The staff document table, other clients' data, and system statistics are never accessible to client accounts under any circumstance.
QR Code Architecture
Four Types of QR Codes

Each QR code serves a specific purpose in the document lifecycle. Here's where to post each one and exactly who scans it.

📱
App Welcome

Welcome QR

Posted at office entrances, bulletin boards, waiting areas. Scanned by new or returning clients — opens this landing page with Register and Sign In options.

📋
OfficeName-sub

Submission QR

Posted at each office counter. Client scans → submission form opens pre-filled with that office's name. Download visible in the Offices section above.

📥
OfficeName-rec

Receiving Station

Posted at the receiving window. Staff scans client's document QR here to mark it Received — office name, officer, and timestamp logged automatically.

✅
OfficeName-rel

Releasing Station

Posted at the pickup window. Scanned when client retrieves their processed document. Closes the document lifecycle with a final travel log entry.

📲 Auto-Created on Staff Registration
When a staff member registers and enters their Office/Unit name, the system automatically generates their full QR code set — no admin action required. Also available manually from the 🖨️ QR Codes page.
→
📋 Office Name Auto-Logged
When a scan happens via an office QR, the office name is parsed from the QR slug and recorded automatically in the document's travel log — no manual entry needed by the scanning officer.
