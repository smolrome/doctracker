System Architecture
Three User Roles
👑Administrator
System Admin
Full system control. Manages all users, oversees every document in the system, and handles all configuration.
  • Manage all staff & client accounts
  • Send staff invitation emails (Brevo API)
  • Generate office QR codes for any office
  • View, add, edit & delete all documents
  • Manual status updates with travel log
  • AI document scanning (Claude API)
🏢Staff
Office Staff
Office personnel who process, receive, and release documents. Each generates their own unique set of office QR codes.
  • Generate own office QR codes (3 types)
  • Add, view & edit documents
  • Update document status via dropdown
  • Scan & upload QR images to log receipt
  • Use office receive/release QR stations
  • Cannot manage user accounts
👤Client
Document Client
External clients who bring documents to the office. Self-registers via posted QR code, submits documents and tracks only their own.
  • Self-registers via office registration QR
  • Submit documents with full details form
  • Receives unique document QR on submission
  • Scans doc QR to mark Received / Released
  • Track own submissions in client portal
  • Cannot view other clients' documents
End-to-End Workflow
Complete Process Flow
PHASE 01
System Setup & Deployment
👑 Admin Only
01.1
🚂
Deploy to Railway
Push code to GitHub. Railway auto-deploys. PostgreSQL is provisioned and linked via DATABASE_URL. Gunicorn serves the Flask app.
01.2
🔑
Configure Environment Variables
Set ADMIN_USERNAME, ADMIN_PASSWORD, CLIENT_REG_CODE, BREVO_API_KEY, MAIL_SENDER, and APP_URL in Railway Variables panel.
01.3
🗄️
Database Initializes
On first run, init_db() automatically creates tables: users, documents (JSONB), invite_tokens, and office_qr_codes with seed data.
01.4
🖨️
Generate & Print Office QR Codes
Each staff member opens 🖨️ QR Codes, types their office name, and gets 3 QR codes formatted as OfficeName-reg, OfficeName-rec, OfficeName-rel.
PHASE 02
Staff Onboarding
🏢 Admin → Staff
02.1
📧
Admin Sends Invitation
Admin goes to 👥 Users → 📧 Send Invite. Enters staff email and name. System generates a cryptographically random 32-char token, stores it with a 48-hour expiry.
02.2
📬
Brevo Email Delivers Link
Brevo HTTPS API delivers a branded HTML email containing the unique registration link: /register?token=abc123... Link is one-time use only.
02.3
📝
Staff Registers via Link
Staff clicks the link. System validates the token is unused and not expired. Registration form shows with email pre-filled. Staff sets username and password. Token is consumed.
02.4
🖨️
Staff Generates Their Office QR Set
Staff logs in, goes to 🖨️ QR Codes, types their office name (e.g. "Ormoc City"), downloads and prints 3 QR codes. Posts them in the correct office locations.
PHASE 03
Client Self-Registration
👤 Client
03.1
📱
Client Scans Registration QR
Client arrives at the office and scans the OfficeName-reg QR posted in the lobby. Opens /client/register with the registration code already embedded in the URL.
03.2
✅
Registration Code Auto-Fills
The CLIENT_REG_CODE is embedded in the QR URL and passed as ?code= parameter. The field is auto-filled and hidden — client only sees name and password fields.
03.3
🎉
Account Created Instantly
Client submits the short form. Account is created with role="client". Password is SHA-256 hashed and stored securely. Redirected to Client Portal login.
03.4
📊
Access Client Portal
Client logs in at /client/login and lands on their personal dashboard. Stats show total submitted, received, and released. Document list is empty until first submission.
PHASE 04
Document Submission
👤 Client
04.1
📄
Client Fills Submission Form
Client taps + Submit and fills: Unit/Office/School/District, Source/Sender name, Content Particulars (document name), Category type, Referred To officer, and optional notes.
04.2
💾
System Logs to Database
System assigns a unique reference (REF-2026-XXXX) and 8-char system ID. Document is inserted into PostgreSQL JSONB documents table with status: "Pending" and initial travel log entry.
04.3
📲
Document QR Generated & Shown
Client is redirected to a confirmation page showing their document QR code immediately. It encodes /receive/<8-char-ID>. Client screenshots or downloads as PNG — this QR is their tracking key.
04.4
👁️
QR Accessible in Dashboard Always
The QR is permanently shown on each document's tracking page in the client portal. Client can tap any submitted document → QR code is displayed with Download PNG and Update Status buttons.
PHASE 05
Document Receiving at Office
👤 Client / Staff
05.1
🚶
Client Arrives at Receiving Window
Client walks to the office receiving window where the OfficeName-rec QR is posted. Staff may also have a tablet open on the receiving station page for shared scanning.
05.2
📷
Scan Document QR
Client opens 📷 Scan QR in their portal — the in-browser camera scanner detects the document QR and auto-redirects to the status update page. Or they open the saved document page directly.
05.3
📥
Tap "Mark as Received"
The doc_scan page opens showing current status "Pending". The blue 📥 Mark as Received button is displayed as the primary action. Client taps it. System validates ownership.
05.4
⚡
Status Updates Instantly
Status: Pending → Received. date_received is logged. Travel log entry added: "Document Received at OfficeName Office via QR scan." Staff dashboard reflects the change in real time.
PHASE 06
Staff Processing & Review
🏢 Staff / Admin
06.1
📊
View & Filter Documents
Staff sees all documents on the main dashboard. Filter by status (Pending, Received, Released, etc.), search by name or reference number, sort by date submitted.
06.2
✏️
Edit Document Details
Staff can edit all fields: add received_by officer, update referred_to, modify routing chain, add notes. All edits use PostgreSQL UPSERT to preserve data integrity.
06.3
⚡
Quick Status Update
Document detail page has a Quick Status Update dropdown — Pending, Received, In Review, In Transit, Released, On Hold, Archived. Each change appends to travel_log with officer name and timestamp.
06.4
🤖
Optional: AI Document Scan
Staff uploads a photo or PDF of a physical document. Claude AI extracts all fields automatically — name, sender, category, dates — and pre-fills the add document form. Cuts manual entry time.
PHASE 07
Document Release & Completion
👤 Client / Staff
07.1
🔔
Document Ready for Pickup
When processing is complete, client is informed and returns to the office. They head to the releasing window where the OfficeName-rel QR is posted.
07.2
📲
Scan the Same Document QR
Client scans their document QR code again — same QR used since submission. Page detects current status is "Received" and displays ✅ Mark as Released as the primary button.
07.3
✅
Tap "Mark as Released"
Client taps the green release button. System updates status to Released, sets date_released, appends a travel log entry with actor name, office name, and timestamp.
07.4
🎉
Document Lifecycle Complete
Client sees a green "Released" confirmation. Full timeline shows: Submitted → Received → Released with all timestamps and actor names. No further QR actions available — cycle is closed.
QR Code Architecture
Four Types of QR Codes
👤
OfficeName-reg

Registration QR

Posted in the office lobby. Client scans → registration page opens with CLIENT_REG_CODE auto-filled and hidden. Client only enters name + password.

📥
OfficeName-rec

Receiving Station QR

Posted at the entrance window. Staff/client scans → opens the office-action receive page where they enter the document ID to mark it Received at that specific office.

✅
OfficeName-rel

Releasing Station QR

Posted at the exit window. Scanned when picking up a processed document. Updates status to Released and logs the office name and timestamp to the travel log.

📄
/receive/<DOC-ID>

Document QR

Unique per document. Generated on submission and shown immediately. Smart page detects current status and shows the appropriate button (Receive or Release). Downloadable as PNG.

📲 Staff Generates QR
Any staff member goes to 🖨️ QR Codes → types their office name → instantly gets 3 office QR codes downloadable as PNG. No admin required.
→
📋 Office Name in Travel Log
When a scan happens via an office QR, the office name is parsed from the QR slug and automatically recorded in the document's travel log entry.
